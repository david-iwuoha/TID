The Problem
Over 60% of Nigeria's Solar Mini-Grids Are Failing Financially
Developers are committing over $200,000 per site using desktop research and guesswork. The question that is never answered before deployment is simple: can this community actually sustain a productive mini-grid? The data to answer it does not exist in any structured, bankable form for rural Nigerian communities. That is the gap STRATOS fills.
💸
$200,000 Committed Per Site
Developers commit this capital at the site selection stage using informal desktop estimates and anecdotal data. No standardised field-verified assessment exists.
📉
60% Financial Failure Rate
Industry analysis confirms the data quality gap at site selection is the primary driver of mini-grid project failure across Nigeria. The technology is not the issue.
🔍
150,000+ Unassessed Communities
The REA has identified over 150,000 communities without reliable electricity. Not one has a standardised viability profile a developer can rely on.
Our Solution
Community Viability Profiles. One Bankable Score.
STRATOS deploys field teams to produce Community Viability Profiles backed by geophysical science, agricultural data, and market economics. Every profile generates a CEPS Score, a 100-point index that works like a credit score for rural energy investment. One number. Bankable. Defensible. Ready for a financier.
A failed mini-grid costs $200,000.
A STRATOS profile costs $5,000. That is 2.5% of the downside it prevents.

Geophysical Assessment

Agricultural Productivity Mapping

What is the CEPS Score?
The Community Energy Potential Score (CEPS) is a 100-point composite index that translates raw field data into a single bankable investment signal. It combines geophysical survey data, agricultural productivity mapping, and market economics into one number a developer can take to a financier. Score above 65 signals a viable investment. Below 50 signals high risk.
Above 65
Viable
Proceed to investment with confidence
50 to 65
Conditional
Mitigate specific risks before proceeding
Below 50
High Risk
Seek alternative site before committing
